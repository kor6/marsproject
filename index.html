Exploration and Evidence

Over the last fifty years, various missions to the red planet have sought to determine the probability of such an evolution.

A habitable environment

Mars wasn't always a desolate wasteland. In the past, water may have flowed across the surface in rivers and streams, and that vast oceans covered the planet..

Are we the Martians?

Some scientists argue that a meteorite from Earth could have traveled to Mars — or vice versa.

Searching for life

Mars is thought to be the best chance of finding evidence of extra-terrestrial life because it once had running water and an atmosphere.

Tiny UFOs

A meteorite found in Antarctica,in 1996 when scientists claimed that it could contain evidence of traces of life on Mars. Known as ALH 84001.

Life on Mars is a real possibility

Scientists found structures on the Nakhla meteorite. They determined that as much as three-fourths of the organic material may not stem from contamination by Earth. However, no DNA was found.
